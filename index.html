About us

Group of young people with opened eyes willing to help others to discover new opportunities in their life through direct experience. We want to educate them at Erasmus+ courses and volunteering.

Most of us are students and we find many of our colleagues struggling with unimportant stuff. We want to open their minds and together with them contribute for better future of us and younger peers.

We are welcoming everyone motivated to join us. If you represent another organization, company or public body, be our partner or sponsor.

Our values

We are following general moral and ethical values. Beside that we indentified other common values:

1) volunteering, because it leads us to humanity and selfless love for others, which is often lacking in our world,

2) history and culture: we are involved in projects rescuing old castles and ruins, preserving traditional values,

3) healthy lifestyle through sport,

4) nature: we put effort on environmental protection through motto "do not waste".

Our vision

We want to live in the world with good relationships betwen diverse cultures and different countries. People should be cultivated active citizens with strong moral values.

Our mission

Providing opportunity to young people to travel, gain new experience abroad, develop their personality through education and volunteering and cultivating young generation in order they have more rational views.

Our objectives

To encourage people to speak more openly and confidentially between each other and to develop their presentation skills.

To develop sense of active citizenship amongs young people.

To maximize social inclusion and to increase tolerance towards different nationalities, religions and people having different skin colours.

To increase employability of young people in the labor market.

To inform young people about possibilities of study stays, courses abroad, internships and voluntary programs.

To promote sport activities and healthy lifestyle among young people.

To increase awareness of human impact on environmental issues and to multiply effort of young people to deal with it in order to protect nature and biodiversity.

To promote active volunteering in order to protect our cultural identity and heritage.

Target group

We decided to target our activities and offers on young people with some obstacles in their life, young leaders and influencers with values corresponding to ours and young people searching for their life fullfillment and self-realization.
